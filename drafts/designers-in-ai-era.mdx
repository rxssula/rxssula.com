---
title: Designers in AI Era
description: AI is changing how everyone work. However only designers are still doing some work in Figma. I think it should change.
date: 2026-06-18
---

I work with designers and I hate when you just turn their design into code. That work when you just didn't do something like they wanted to. I mean they are not wrong. Of course developers should do their work so that the website will look just like designer wanted to. However I think this workflow should change.

AI is changing how people work. But what I noticed is that designers are still doing their work like they always did.
There should be workflow where designers can just turn their design into code. There is Figma MCP but to use that you should have the premium subscription. There should be a tool that is free, opensource and easy to use.
